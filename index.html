About me

I'm Vinicius Murari, a Brazilian-born photographer, filmmaker, and digital designer now calling Dublin, Ireland home. My work lives at the intersection of technical precision and authentic storytelling.

Whether it's the quiet emotion of a wedding morning, the texture of a perfectly lit dish, the energy of a corporate launch — or a cinematic aerial shot of Ireland's coastline — I approach every project with the same care: genuine connection and obsessive attention to light.

Based in Ranelagh, Dublin, I work across Ireland and travel internationally for the right project. If you have a vision, let's make it real.

FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings, I recommend booking 6-12 months ahead. For portraits, corporate events and other sessions, 2-4 weeks is usually sufficient, but earlier is always better to guarantee availability.

How long until I receive my photos?

Turnaround time is typically 2-3 weeks for portraits and events, and 4-6 weeks for weddings. Rush delivery is available on request.

Do you travel outside Dublin?

Absolutely! I cover all of Ireland and travel internationally for destination weddings and projects. Travel fees may apply depending on distance.

What's included in a photography session?

Every session includes a pre-shoot consultation, the photo session itself, professional editing of all selected images, and delivery via a private online gallery. Prints and albums are available as add-ons.

Can I choose which photos get edited?

Yes! After the session, you'll receive a curated selection of the best shots. You can request edits, adjustments or additional photos from the full set.

Do you offer videography as well?

Yes, I offer photography and videography services including drone/aerial footage. Combo packages are available at a better rate than booking separately.
